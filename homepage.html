ABOUT THE GAME


Through an interactive story, experience what life on Earth may become based on your actions that have a direct effect on the environment.


Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to raise awareness and educate people of all ages about the impact they have on their surrounding environment.


VISION: Our vision is to inspire our players to take what they have learned from our game about climate change and implement their ideas of solutions into their communities.

INTRUCTIONS

Journey through a text adventure where you decisions affect the world's environment with the click of a button.
